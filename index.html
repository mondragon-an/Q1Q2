★── Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse ──★



"Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse" is the sequel of "Spider-Man: Into The Spider-Verse". After the main character Miles Morales reunites with Gwen Stacy, he is thrown actross the Multiverse where Miles encounters a team of Spider-People from other universes. However, when the heros clash on how to handle a new threat, Miles finds himself against other Spider-People. Miles must soon redefine what it means to be a hero so he can save the people he cares about
